Buttermilk Pancakes

Some sort of lovely description about the best buttermilk pancakes in the world.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Melted butter
  • 2 tbsp Whole milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 cup All purpose flour
  • 2 tsp Baking powder
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Instructions

  1. Melt butter
  2. Beat lightly: milk, butter, egg
  3. Add: flour, baking powder, sugar, salt
  4. Mix
  5. Melt a little butter in the pan
  6. Drop batter by tablespoon into the pan
  7. Turn pancakes over when the top is full of little holes and teh base is golden
